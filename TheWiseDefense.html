The Wise Defense

  • Project date: December, 2022
  • Trailer: The Wise Defense trailer
  • Project URL: https://gliwisestudio.itch.io/the-wise-defense

"The Wise Defense" is a card based Tower Defense, where the player has to manage a randomized deck of cards each turn to protect its towers against multiple enemy hordes.
The game was made with WebGL and is available to play from browser on Itch.io on both PC and mobile devices.
I worked as a programmer on this project, mainly on the enemies and cards, as well as the login functionality. I also worked on some other aspects such as some of the menus and optimization.
